# SEO/GEO Source Of Truth

## Priority

Launch priority:

1. Essentuki.
2. Kislovodsk.
3. Zheleznovodsk.
4. Pyatigorsk.

Essentuki is the first commercial and operational focus. KMV is the wider service area.

## Page Intent

The MVP should target commercial local intent:

- ремонт квартир Ессентуки;
- отделочные работы Ессентуки;
- ремонт ванной Ессентуки;
- ремонт санузла Ессентуки;
- плиточные работы Ессентуки;
- электрика Ессентуки as a small test;
- строительство домов Ессентуки as a cautious test.

## Structure Rules

- First screen must mention Essentuki directly.
- KMV can be present in service area and footer.
- Do not start with a generic KMV-wide message.
- Service sections should preserve the city/service relationship.
- Future city pages must not be doorway spam; each city needs local copy, service priorities and operational feasibility.

## Future Rollout

After Essentuki validation:

- create/adapt Kislovodsk landing or section;
- then Zheleznovodsk;
- then Pyatigorsk.

Each rollout should be tied to real operations, ad results and ability to handle measurement visits.

## SEO Copy Rules

- Use readable Russian, not keyword stuffing.
- Put local phrase in H1/hero and relevant section headings.
- Use structured service names.
- Add FAQ only when answers are real and useful.
- Add privacy policy if form collects phone/name.
- Use local business schema only after real company/contact data is approved.
