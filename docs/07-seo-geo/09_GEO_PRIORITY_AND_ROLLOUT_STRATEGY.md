# 09. GEO Priority and Rollout Strategy

## Strategic decision

The project must be built as an Essentuki-first construction and finishing service.

Primary geography:

1. Essentuki — main base, first advertising focus, strongest site positioning.
2. Kislovodsk — second-stage expansion after the Essentuki offer and lead funnel are tested.
3. Zheleznovodsk — third-stage expansion; smaller geography, useful for additional leads and lower competition testing.
4. Pyatigorsk — fourth-stage expansion; larger and more competitive market, launch only after proof of conversion, case materials, and stable contractor workflow.

This order is intentional. The first objective is not to cover the whole KMW region immediately, but to win trust and generate predictable leads in the base city.

## Why Essentuki first

Essentuki should be treated as the operational center of the business.

Advantages:

- easier and faster site visits;
- lower logistics cost for free measurement visits;
- easier quality control during work;
- stronger local trust in sales communication;
- better chance to build first cases, photos, reviews, and referrals;
- lower risk than trying to advertise across the whole KMW region at once.

## Core positioning for Essentuki

Recommended positioning:

> Repair and finishing works in Essentuki with free measurement, transparent estimate, contractor selection, and construction quality control.

The project should not present itself only as a generic repair crew. The more defensible role is:

- client acquisition;
- consultation;
- measurement;
- estimate preparation;
- contractor and brigade selection;
- work coordination;
- quality control;
- client communication throughout the project.

## City rollout stages

### Stage 1 — Essentuki MVP

Goal: prove that the landing page and advertising funnel can generate real requests.

Required:

- one strong landing page focused on Essentuki;
- local copy: Essentuki, nearby settlements, fast measurement visit;
- lead form with city/object type/area/task;
- simple repair estimate calculator or request-based calculator;
- Yandex Direct test only for Essentuki commercial queries;
- first case collection process;
- contractor shortlist and basic agreement rules.

Launch services:

- apartment finishing;
- turnkey apartment repair;
- bathroom/toilet repair;
- electrical works;
- private house finishing;
- small construction-related works if reliable contractors are available.

### Stage 2 — Kislovodsk

Launch after Essentuki has at least:

- confirmed lead flow;
- at least 1–3 completed/active projects or strong portfolio substitutes;
- clear lead qualification script;
- predictable measurement/sales process.

Kislovodsk page should be created as a separate geo page, not only as a city mention.

### Stage 3 — Zheleznovodsk

Zheleznovodsk can be used as a smaller expansion market. The offer may emphasize:

- finishing of apartments and houses;
- repair in resort/private property;
- small and medium objects;
- free visit and estimate by appointment.

### Stage 4 — Pyatigorsk

Pyatigorsk should not be the first advertising battlefield.

Reasons:

- higher visible competition;
- larger number of aggregators and repair companies;
- likely higher click cost;
- more difficult differentiation without cases and reviews.

Pyatigorsk launch requires stronger trust assets:

- cases;
- before/after photos;
- reviews;
- clearer guarantee terms;
- more polished landing or separate city page;
- stronger budget.

## Site structure implications

The first version should not be a generic KMW site. It should be:

- main landing: Essentuki-first;
- secondary mentions: Kislovodsk, Zheleznovodsk, Pyatigorsk as service area / expansion area;
- future architecture: separate SEO/ad landing pages by city.

Recommended URL model later:

- `/` — Essentuki-focused main page;
- `/kislovodsk` — future city landing;
- `/zheleznovodsk` — future city landing;
- `/pyatigorsk` — future city landing after proof of concept;
- `/services/remont-kvartir`;
- `/services/otdelochnye-raboty`;
- `/services/elektrika`;
- `/services/krovelnye-raboty`.

For MVP, the project can start with a single landing page and later split into city/service pages.

## Advertising implications

Do not spend the first budget across all KMW cities.

Initial test:

- Yandex Search only;
- Essentuki only;
- exact and phrase commercial queries;
- no broad KMW campaign in the first test;
- no Pyatigorsk campaign at launch;
- separate campaigns by service group.

Suggested first test groups:

1. ремонт квартир Ессентуки;
2. ремонт квартир под ключ Ессентуки;
3. отделочные работы Ессентуки;
4. ремонт ванной Ессентуки;
5. электрик / электромонтажные работы Ессентуки;
6. ремонт дома / отделка дома Ессентуки.

## Practical success criteria

The Essentuki stage can be considered validated if within 30–60 days the project gets:

- stable relevant calls/forms;
- at least several measurement visits;
- at least one paid contract or strong near-contract negotiation;
- clear understanding of lead cost;
- list of converting and non-converting keywords;
- updated offer based on real objections.
