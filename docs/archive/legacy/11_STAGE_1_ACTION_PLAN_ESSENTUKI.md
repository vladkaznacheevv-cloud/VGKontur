# 11. Stage 1 Action Plan — Essentuki-first Launch

## Stage goal

Prepare the project for a low-budget validation launch in Essentuki.

The goal is not to build a perfect construction company website. The goal is to test whether the offer can generate real paid leads and whether the team can process those leads into measurements and contracts.

## Work block 1 — Brand and offer

Decide:

- project name;
- phone / WhatsApp / Telegram;
- whether the project uses a personal founder face or a company-like brand;
- guarantee wording;
- whether contracts are signed as individual / self-employed / LLC / partner contractor;
- exact wording for “construction control”.

Deliverables:

- brand placeholder;
- hero offer;
- short company description;
- trust bullets;
- legal-safe claims list.

## Work block 2 — Competitor and offer matrix

Create a table for Essentuki competitors:

- company / site / platform;
- main offer;
- price claims;
- guarantee claims;
- free measurement claim;
- portfolio availability;
- weaknesses;
- how we differentiate.

Primary competitor types:

- city landing networks;
- aggregators;
- private masters;
- local companies;
- design studios.

## Work block 3 — Yandex demand check

Manual checks required:

- Yandex Wordstat by Essentuki;
- Yandex Direct budget forecast;
- commercial keywords by city;
- negative keyword list;
- approximate CPC and click volume.

Priority keywords:

- ремонт квартир ессентуки;
- ремонт квартир под ключ ессентуки;
- отделочные работы ессентуки;
- ремонт ванной ессентуки;
- ремонт дома ессентуки;
- электрик ессентуки;
- кровельные работы ессентуки.

Do not rely only on broad “КМВ” queries.

## Work block 4 — Landing page MVP

Prepare:

- one-page structure;
- texts;
- calculator logic;
- lead form;
- FAQ;
- privacy policy;
- disclaimer / guarantee limitations;
- thank-you screen;
- simple lead delivery to WhatsApp/Telegram/email/Google Sheets.

## Work block 5 — Contractor readiness

Before advertising, confirm:

- who goes to measurements;
- who calculates estimates;
- who performs finishing works;
- who performs electrical works;
- who performs roofing;
- who handles stonework;
- payment scheme with brigades;
- who is responsible for defects;
- minimum project size worth taking.

## Work block 6 — First ad test

Recommended test budget:

- 20,000–40,000 RUB for the first 30 days if possible;
- smaller test possible, but data may be weak;
- do not split the first test across all cities.

Campaign structure:

- Search only;
- Essentuki only;
- separate ad groups by service;
- no automatic broad expansion at launch;
- calls and WhatsApp clicks tracked;
- form submissions tracked.

## Work block 7 — Metrics

Track weekly:

- ad spend;
- impressions;
- clicks;
- CTR;
- CPC;
- calls;
- WhatsApp clicks;
- form submissions;
- qualified leads;
- measurements scheduled;
- measurements completed;
- estimates sent;
- contracts signed;
- revenue;
- margin.

## Decision after 30–60 days

If Essentuki produces qualified leads:

- improve landing;
- add real cases;
- expand to Kislovodsk;
- create separate Kislovodsk landing;
- increase budget gradually.

If there are leads but no contracts:

- fix sales script;
- fix estimate process;
- analyze price objections;
- check contractor reliability;
- adjust offer.

If there are no qualified leads:

- check keywords;
- check ad copy;
- check landing relevance;
- narrow the offer;
- test service-specific pages, e.g. bathroom repair or finishing works.
