# Design Source Of Truth

## Approved Direction

**Direction A - Clean Local Expert / Desktop**

This is the approved visual direction for the MVP website for construction, renovation and finishing services across KMV, with the first launch focused on Essentuki.

## Brand Identity

- Main brand name: **VG Контур**.
- Header/logo variant: **VG КОНТУР**.
- Descriptor: **Ремонт и отделка по КМВ**.
- Slogan: **Ремонт без хаоса: замер, смета, контроль**.
- Main hero/offer must remain focused on renovation and finishing in Essentuki.

## Logo System

Approved logo direction: a VG monogram made from architectural plan / room-contour lines. The logo should suggest planning, precision, estimate clarity, control and neat finishing.

Source SVG files:

- Use the primary VG Plan Mark in the MVP header and footer.
- Do not use helmets, hammers, bricks, fake 3D, complex gradients or heavy illustration inside the logo.
- Keep the mark thin, architectural and compatible with the warm minimal Direction A style.

## Visual Description

The site should feel like a clean, reliable local expert:

- light warm minimalism;
- practical construction/renovation company aesthetic;
- calm and trustworthy;
- commercial enough for Yandex Direct traffic;
- local and concrete, not abstract or agency-like.

## Colors

Use this color logic, then refine during implementation:

- background: off-white / warm white;
- section surfaces: warm stone beige;
- primary text: graphite;
- secondary text: warm gray;
- primary accent: muted green;
- secondary accent: deep blue if needed;
- dividers: light warm gray;
- shadows: soft and subtle.

Do not let the page become a one-note beige layout. Use graphite, white space and restrained green/blue accents for hierarchy.

## UI Principles

- Clear first screen with strong local offer.
- Precise grid and predictable spacing.
- Cards are allowed for services and compact information blocks.
- Use restrained icons, thin dividers and soft shadows.
- CTAs must be visible and practical.
- Calculator must feel like a lead-generation tool, not a legal estimate.
- Forms must be simple and built for Telegram-group processing.
- Mobile adaptation must be designed deliberately, not just collapsed from desktop.

## Required Page Blocks

- Header.
- Hero / first screen.
- Services grid.
- Trust section.
- Process timeline.
- Preliminary calculator.
- Lead form.
- Contacts and KMV service area.
- Footer.

Detailed block requirements: `../05-site-architecture/SITE_BLOCKS_AND_CONTENT_REQUIREMENTS.md`.

## Do Not Use

- luxury real estate style;
- cheap construction template style;
- dark brutalist layouts;
- neon/crypto/fintech visual language;
- excessive 3D;
- fake awards;
- fake reviews;
- close-up faces;
- dirty construction sites;
- unrealistic AI hands;
- oversized decorative gradients or abstract blobs;
- unreadable placeholder text.

## Mobile Adaptation

- First screen must keep the offer, city and CTA visible.
- Header should prioritize phone and messenger contact.
- Service cards should stack cleanly with main launch services first.
- Timeline should become vertical.
- Calculator should use simple stacked fields.
- Buttons and form fields must be comfortable for thumb input.
- Avoid dense desktop text blocks on mobile.

## Future Implementation Requirements

- Preserve the approved direction, but do not copy the mockup pixel-perfectly.
- Use the approved brand **VG Контур** and the primary SVG logo unless a later brand decision replaces it.
- Replace phone and contact data only when approved.
- Do not show fake email if email is not used.
- Use real contact and privacy data before paid launch.
- Keep Essentuki as the primary message while preserving KMV as the service area.
- Ensure images and icons match the warm clean construction aesthetic.
