## Why this direction is approved

The mockup matches the current project strategy:

- light warm tones;
- local construction and renovation service positioning;
- strong Essentuki-first hero offer;
- simple conversion-focused layout;
- service cards for main and secondary directions;
- trust section with specialists, quality control, staged workflow and guarantee;
- visible process timeline;
- calculator block;
- lead form;
- contacts and KMV service area;
- practical, trustworthy style without luxury real-estate excess or cheap construction template aesthetics.

## Implementation guidance for Stage 4

Use the reference as the primary design benchmark, not as a pixel-perfect requirement. The production site should preserve:

1. Light off-white / warm stone visual base.
2. Graphite typography.
3. Muted green accent for CTA, icons, checkmarks and selected UI states.
4. Interior/renovation hero visual with clean apartment atmosphere.
5. Large clear first-screen headline.
6. Compact trust bullets.
7. Card-based service grid.
8. Timeline-style “Как мы работаем”.
9. Calculator card with simple fields.
10. Lead form oriented to Telegram-group processing.
11. Contacts block with phone, WhatsApp, Telegram and KMV service zone.

## Important corrections before production

During Stage 4, the mockup should be adapted to real project data:

- replace placeholder brand/logo after naming decision;
- replace placeholder phone with real contact data once approved;
- avoid fake email if email is not used;
- avoid making claims that cannot be operationally guaranteed;
- keep focus on Essentuki while preserving KMV as service zone;
- ensure mobile-first adaptation is designed separately, not automatically derived from desktop;
- keep the calculator as lead-generation logic, not an exact legal estimate;
- forms should send to Telegram group, not CRM at launch.
