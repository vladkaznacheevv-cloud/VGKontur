# Stage 3.5 — Proposed Design Directions

Goal: choose 1 visual direction before frontend production.

## Direction A — Clean Local Expert

### Essence
A clean light landing page for a reliable local renovation operator in Essentuki.

### Visual mood
Bright, honest, precise, practical, trustworthy. Not luxury, not cheap.

### Palette
- background: warm off-white;
- cards: white / light stone;
- text: graphite;
- accent: muted green or deep construction blue;
- secondary accent: sand / beige.

### Hero
Left side:
- large headline;
- short subheadline;
- CTAs;
- trust bullets.

Right side:
- realistic bright interior / renovation process image;
- small overlay card with “Бесплатный замер”, “Смета”, “Контроль качества”.

### Best for
Maximum conversion, Yandex Direct traffic, local trust, fastest implementation.

### Weakness
Less “wow” than architectural editorial concepts.

### Recommendation
Best first MVP option.

---

## Direction B — Architectural Editorial

### Essence
A more premium, architecture-studio-like site: large typography, asymmetric grid, spacious layout, high-quality interior/construction visuals.

### Visual mood
Premium, calm, editorial, modern, architectural.

### Palette
- white;
- limestone;
- warm grey;
- graphite;
- muted terracotta or olive accent.

### Hero
Large typographic composition:
“Ремонт и отделочные работы в Ессентуках”
with a structured visual collage:
- apartment interior;
- construction detail;
- measurement/smeta card;
- process status.

### Best for
Strong visual impression, differentiation from typical repair sites, future brand scaling across KMV.

### Weakness
Needs more careful mobile adaptation and stronger copy so the site does not feel too abstract.

### Recommendation
Good as a premium version after MVP or as the chosen visual direction if we want higher perceived value.

---

## Direction C — Soft Construction Tech

### Essence
A light, modern, slightly technical site with calculator-first logic, blueprint lines, bento cards and process control emphasis.

### Visual mood
Systematic, engineered, controlled, modern, slightly high-tech but not fintech.

### Palette
- light grey;
- white;
- graphite;
- muted blue;
- blueprint blue accents;
- sand tone for warmth.

### Hero
Hero headline + embedded mini-calculator / estimate widget:
- object type;
- area;
- work type;
- city;
- CTA: “Получить предварительный расчёт”.

### Best for
Advertising traffic that wants price quickly, differentiating through process and control, future online calculator.

### Weakness
May feel colder if there are not enough human/trust elements.

### Recommendation
Strong alternative if calculator is central from day one.

---

## Recommended approval path

1. Generate visual preview for Direction A.
2. Generate visual preview for Direction B.
3. Generate visual preview for Direction C.
4. Choose one primary direction.
5. Polish only the selected direction.
6. Prepare frontend handoff for Stage 4.

Recommended first choice for launch:

> Direction A — Clean Local Expert

Reason:
It is the safest balance of trust, speed, clarity, mobile-first UX and Yandex Direct conversion.
